__Names__
 * randomname / rname -- Generates a name procedurally with quite wild outcomes
 * malename /mname -- Picks a male name from a list of 5k+ names from American records
 * femalename /fname -- Picks a female name from a list of 5k+ names from American records
 * surname /sname -- Picks a surname from a list of 5k+ names from American records

__NPC__
 * fantasyNPC / fNPC [(Job)=random] -- Procedurally generates a fantasy character with shown (Name, Gendre, Age, Job and Characteristic) and hidden proprieties (Trait, Secret). You can input a set job

__Inn__
 * fantasyinn / finn / inn -- Procedurally generates a fantasy inn with shown (Name, Oddity, Age, and Specialty) and hidden proprieties (Secret) as well as an owner and waiter.

__Mission__
 * mission / mis / m [(difficulty)=random] -- Procedurally generates a mission with shown (Difficulty, Objectives, Rewards and Complications) and hidden proprieties (Secret complications). Higher difficulties have  more objectives and complications as well as higher rewards. A difficulty can be set manually.
 * jobboard / jb [(number)=5] -- Procedurally generates a list of missions with shown (Difficulty, Objectives, Rewards and Complications) and hidden proprieties (Secret complications). You input the number of missions with a default of five.

__Settlement__
 * fantasysettlement / fsettlement / fset [(size)=random] -- Procedurally generates a settlement with shown (Size(Outpost/Hamlet/Village/Town/City), Name, Population, Asset, Oddity, a ruler(character/or conciel) and at least one institution and hidden proprieties (Age, problem, more info on ruler, more info on institutions).
 * fantasyoutpost / foutpost -- Procedurally generates a outpost with shown (Size, Name, Population, Asset, Oddity, a ruler(character/or council) and one institution and hidden proprieties (Age, problem, more info on ruler, more info on institution).
 * fantasyhamlet / fhamlet -- Procedurally generates a hamlet with shown (Size, Name, Population, Asset, Oddity, a ruler(character/or council) and at least one institution and hidden proprieties (Age, problem, more info on ruler, more info on institutions).
 * fantasyvillage / fvillage -- Procedurally generates a village with shown (Size, Name, Population, Asset, Oddity, a ruler(character/or council) and at least one institution and hidden proprieties (Age, problem, more info on ruler, more info on institutions).
 * fantasytown / ftown -- Procedurally generates a town with shown (Size, Name, Population, Asset, Oddity, a ruler(character/or council) and at least one institution and hidden proprieties (Age, problem, more info on ruler, more info on institutions).
 * fantasycity / fcity -- Procedurally generates a city with shown (Size, Name, Population, Asset, Oddity, a ruler(character/or council) and at least one institution and hidden proprieties (Age, problem, more info on ruler, more info on institutions).
